Diva Cakes is your number one cake shop in Lagos, Nigeria

We are located in the business capital of Nigeria, Lagos. We have our outlets in the following areas of Lagos; Opebi, Surulere, Gbagada, Akowonjo, Victoria Island, Lekki and Osapa London.

In all our shops, we stock ready for pick-up and made-to-order sumptuous, freshly baked cakes and other confectioneries like assorted desserts, Cookies, Chin Chin, Hot bread and specialty products like Diva Mix, Diva cream, brown bubble double delight, red velvet, cheese cakes, chocolate magic.

Our cakes come in a variety of flavours like chocolate, strawberry, butterscotch, coconut, banana, Irish cream etc. You also have the choice of various shapes, colors, sizes, characters and creative designs

Our unique tastes, designs and value for money offerings stand us out from the pack and make our numerous private and corporate customers across Nigeria keep coming back to us.

We are open Mondays through Saturdays from 8am to 6pm, and on Sundays from 9am-3pm (in select outlets).
